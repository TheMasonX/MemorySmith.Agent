## Notes

Arrows are crafted from 1 flint + 1 stick + 1 feather → 4 arrows.
They are ammunition for bows and crossbows. Skeletons drop 0–2 arrows
on death as an alternative source.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Dig gravel → flint
2. Craft sticks from planks
3. Hunt chickens → feathers
4. Craft 1 flint + 1 stick + 1 feather → 4 arrows

**Alternative source:** Kill skeletons — they drop 0–2 arrows each.
Skeleton farms provide an unlimited renewable arrow supply.

**Related items:** `bow`, `flint`, `stick`, `feather`, `skeleton`
