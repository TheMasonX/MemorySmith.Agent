## Notes

Obsidian is formed when water flows over a lava source block. It requires a
diamond pickaxe or better to mine (min_harvest_level: 4). Mining without the
correct tool takes over 250 seconds per block and drops nothing.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Find a lava source pool
2. Place water source over lava → obsidian forms at the contact point
3. Mine with diamond pickaxe (9.4 seconds per block)

**Key uses:**
- Enchanting table crafting (4 obsidian required)
- Nether portal frame (10 obsidian minimum)
- Beacon base, ender chest crafting
- Blast-resistant building material (highest resistance in vanilla)

**Related items:** `enchanting_table` (crafted with obsidian), `crying_obsidian`
(decorative variant, non-renewable from ruined portals)
