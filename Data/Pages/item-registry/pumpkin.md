## Notes

Pumpkins grow from pumpkin seeds planted on farmland. They spawn on adjacent
dirt/grass blocks when the stem matures. Pumpkins can be mined with any tool
(axe is fastest) and are used for decoration, jack-o-lanterns, and pumpkin pie.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Find pumpkin seeds in chests (mineshafts, dungeons) or trade with villagers
2. Plant on farmland, wait for stem growth and pumpkin generation
3. Mine the pumpkin (leaves the stem intact for regrowth)

**Crafting uses:**
- 1 pumpkin + 1 torch → 1 jack_o_lantern
- 1 pumpkin + 1 egg + 1 sugar → 1 pumpkin_pie
- 4 pumpkin seeds from 1 pumpkin
- 1 pumpkin → carved_pumpkin (with shears)

**Related items:** `pumpkin_seeds`, `jack_o_lantern`, `pumpkin_pie`, `carved_pumpkin`
