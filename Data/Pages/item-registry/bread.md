## Notes

Bread is crafted from 3 wheat in a horizontal row. It is a simple, reliable
food source that restores 5 hunger points (2.5 shanks).

**Gather strategy:**
1. Farm wheat (plant seeds, grow, harvest)
2. Craft 3 wheat → 1 bread

**Nutrition:**
- Hunger restored: 5 (2.5 shanks)
- Saturation: 6.0

Bread is not the most efficient food (steak/porkchop is better) but it is
the easiest to mass-produce from wheat farms. Excellent early-game staple.

**Related items:** `wheat`, `wheat_seeds`, `hay_bale`
