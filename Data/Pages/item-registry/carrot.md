## Notes

Carrots are a crop/food item found in village farms or dropped by zombies
(rare drop). Plant carrots on farmland to grow more. Each mature plant drops
1–4 carrots.

**Gather strategy:**
- Loot village farms (most reliable source)
- Kill zombies (rare ~2.5% drop)
- Plant and grow on farmland for renewable supply

**Nutrition:**
- Hunger restored: 3 (1.5 shanks)
- Saturation: 4.8

**Uses:**
- Food source (eaten raw)
- Pig breeding and attraction
- Rabbit breeding
- Crafting golden_carrot (1 carrot + 8 gold_nuggets)

**Related items:** `golden_carrot`, `potato`, `beetroot`, `wheat`
