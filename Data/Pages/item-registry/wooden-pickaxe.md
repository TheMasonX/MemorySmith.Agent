## Notes

A wooden pickaxe is crafted from 3 planks + 2 sticks. It is the first tier
of pickaxe, capable of mining stone and coal ore. It is the minimum tool
required to start the tool progression.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Punch a tree → oak_log → craft planks and sticks
2. Craft 3 planks + 2 sticks → 1 wooden_pickaxe

**Stats:**
- Mining level: 1 (can mine stone → cobblestone, coal_ore)
- Durability: 59 uses
- Mining speed: 2

**Tier progression:** Wood → Stone → Iron → Diamond → Netherite.
Wooden pickaxes are quick to craft but wear out fast. Mine 3 cobblestone
and upgrade to a stone pickaxe immediately.

**Related items:** `stick`, `oak_planks`, `stone_pickaxe`
