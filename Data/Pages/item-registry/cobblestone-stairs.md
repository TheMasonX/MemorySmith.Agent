## Notes

Cobblestone stairs are crafted from 6 cobblestone (L-shape arrangement) →
4 stairs. They provide rough stone access steps for medieval and survival builds.

**Gather strategy:** Mine cobblestone → craft into stairs.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Access steps for castle walls and towers
- Mine entrance staircases
- Decorative stepped walls

**Related items:** `cobblestone`, `stone_brick_stairs`, `stone_stairs`
