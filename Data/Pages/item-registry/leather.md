## Notes

Leather is obtained by killing cows, mooshrooms, horses, llamas, or hoglins.
It is the primary material for book crafting and leather armour. 4 rabbit
hides can also be crafted into 1 leather.

**Gather strategy:**
- Hunt cows or other leather-dropping mobs
- Each adult cow drops 0–2 leather
- Looting enchantment increases drop count
- Alternatively, fish for leather as a treasure item

**Crafting uses:**
- 1 leather + 3 paper → 1 book
- Leather armour set (helmet, chestplate, leggings, boots)
- Item frame (8 sticks + 1 leather)

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Book crafting for bookshelves (wizard's tower: ~66 books = ~22 leather)
- Item frame crafting for decorative storage

**Related items:** `book`, `bookshelf`, `rabbit_hide`, `item_frame`
