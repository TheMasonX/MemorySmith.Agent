## Notes

Paper is crafted from sugar cane: 3 sugar cane (in a row) → 3 paper.
It is an intermediate material for book crafting and map-making.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Find sugar cane growing near water in any biome
2. Break sugar cane (grows 1–4 blocks tall, break above the bottom for regrowth)
3. Craft 3 sugar cane → 3 paper

**Crafting uses:**
- 3 paper + 1 leather → 1 book
- 8 paper + 1 compass → 1 empty_map
- 3 paper → 1 banner_pattern (for some patterns)

**Phase 4b limitation:** Crafting is manual; CraftItemTool is Phase 5.
Sugar cane farming is Phase 4b (planting, waiting for growth, harvesting).

**Related items:** `sugar_cane` (raw material), `book` (crafted with paper + leather),
`map` (crafted with paper + compass), `sugar` (smelted from sugar cane)
