## Notes

A cobblestone wall is crafted from 6 cobblestone (2×3 arrangement) →
6 cobblestone walls. Walls function like fences but are 1.5 blocks tall
and made of stone — players can stand on top of them.

**Gather strategy:** Mine cobblestone → craft 6 cobblestone → 6 walls.

**Placement note:** Walls connect to adjacent walls and solid blocks,
similar to fences. They are taller than fences and cannot be jumped over.
Mobs cannot jump over walls.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Castle perimeter walls and defensive barriers
- Garden and courtyard boundaries
- Decorative column surrounds

**Related items:** `cobblestone`, `oak_fence`, `stone_brick_wall`
