## Notes

Sandstone is crafted from 4 sand (2×2 arrangement) → 1 sandstone. It is a
decorative yellow building block commonly found in desert temples and villages.

**Gather strategy:** Dig sand and craft 4 sand → 1 sandstone at a crafting table.
Sandstone is also found naturally in desert biomes as part of the terrain.

**Variants:** sandstone_stairs, sandstone_slab, sandstone_wall, chiseled_sandstone,
cut_sandstone, smooth_sandstone.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Desert-themed builds
- Pyramid and temple construction
- Warm-toned decorative walls and paths

**Related items:** `sand`, `sandstone_stairs`, `sandstone_slab`, `red_sandstone`
