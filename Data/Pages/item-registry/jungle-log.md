## Notes

Jungle logs are obtained by chopping jungle trees, found in jungle biomes.
Jungle trees are the tallest in the game, growing up to 30 blocks high,
and yield large amounts of logs per tree.

**Gather strategy:** Chop jungle tree trunks. 2×2 jungle saplings grow
the tallest trees. Jungle logs have a brown bark with a lighter interior.

**Crafting:** 1 jungle_log → 4 jungle_planks.
All logs are interchangeable for plank crafting.

**Special:** Jungle logs are the only wood type required for cocoa bean
farming (place cocoa beans on jungle log side).

**Related items:** `jungle_planks`, `cocoa_beans`, `oak_log`
