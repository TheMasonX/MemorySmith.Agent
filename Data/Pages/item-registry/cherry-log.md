## Notes

Cherry logs are obtained by chopping cherry trees (Java 1.20+), found in cherry
grove biomes. Cherry trees have pink leaves and dark bark, and drop pink petals.

**Gather strategy:** Chop cherry tree trunks in cherry grove biomes.
Cherry trees are medium height with distinctive pink foliage.

**Crafting:** 1 cherry_log → 4 cherry_planks.
All logs are interchangeable for plank crafting.

**Related items:** `cherry_planks`, `cherry_leaves`, `pink_petals`
