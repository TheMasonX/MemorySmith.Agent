## Notes

Spruce planks are crafted from spruce logs: 1 spruce_log → 4 spruce_planks.
They have a darker brown colour than oak planks, giving a rustic/magical aesthetic.

**Gather strategy:** Chop spruce_log from spruce trees (taiga biomes),
then craft into planks.

**Phase 4b limitation:** Crafting is manual; CraftItemTool is Phase 5.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Interior flooring (wizard's tower: spruce plank floors in upper levels)
- Bookshelf backing and decorative panelling
- Crafting: spruce stairs, slabs, doors, fences, trapdoors
