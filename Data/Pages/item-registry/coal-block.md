## Notes

A block of coal is crafted from 9 coal (3×3 arrangement). It can be used
as compact fuel storage or as a decorative black block. A coal block smelts
80 items in a furnace (equivalent to 10 individual coal pieces — 20% more
efficient than using 9 separate coal).

**Gather strategy:** Craft 9 coal → 1 coal_block. Uncraftable back to 9 coal.

**Fuel efficiency:** 1 coal_block = 80 smelts (vs 72 smelts for 9 individual coal).
Always use coal blocks for large smelting operations.

**Common uses:**
- Compact fuel storage for furnace arrays
- Decorative black building block
- Beacon base material

**Related items:** `coal`, `charcoal`, `iron_block`, `gold_block`, `diamond_block`
