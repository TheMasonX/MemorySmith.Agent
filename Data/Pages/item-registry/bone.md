## Notes

Bones are obtained by killing skeletons (0–2 bones per kill). They are used
for bone meal (fertiliser) and taming/wolf health.

**Gather strategy:** Hunt skeletons at night or in caves. Skeletons also
spawn in soul sand valleys in the Nether. Looting enchantment increases drops.

**Crafting:**
- 1 bone → 3 bone_meal (crafted at any crafting grid position)
- 9 bone_meal → 1 bone_block (compact storage)

**Related items:** `bone_meal` (crafted from bone), `skeleton`, `arrow`
(skeletons drop arrows)
