## Notes

Shears are crafted from 2 iron ingots. They are used to shear sheep for wool
(without killing them), collect leaves, cobwebs, and other fragile blocks.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Mine iron_ore → smelt to iron_ingot (FurnaceTool, Phase 4b)
2. Craft 2 iron_ingots → 1 shears

**Key uses:**
- Shear sheep → 1–3 wool (sheep regrow wool by eating grass)
- Collect leaves without them decaying (for decoration)
- Harvest cobwebs → 1 string (faster than sword)
- Harvest honeycomb from bee nests

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Wool collection for bed crafting (all bed blueprints)
- Decorative leaf collection for landscaping
- String collection from cobwebs

**Related items:** `iron_ingot`, `white_wool`, `string`
