## Notes

Eggs are laid by chickens every 5–15 minutes. They can be thrown (right-click)
and have a 1/8 chance to spawn a baby chicken. Eggs are used for cakes and
pumpkin pies.

**Gather strategy:**
- Capture and breed chickens in a pen
- Chickens lay eggs periodically on any surface
- Collect eggs from the ground

**Crafting uses:**
- 3 wheat + 1 sugar + 1 egg + 2 milk_bucket + 1 cocoa_beans → 1 cake
- 1 pumpkin + 1 sugar + 1 egg → 1 pumpkin_pie

**Related items:** `chicken`, `cake`, `pumpkin_pie`
