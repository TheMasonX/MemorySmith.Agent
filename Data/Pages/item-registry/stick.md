## Notes

Sticks are crafted from planks: 2 planks → 4 sticks. They are a fundamental
crafting component used across dozens of recipes.

**Gather strategy:** Mine any wood log → 4 planks → 4 sticks (per 2 planks).
Sticks can also be obtained from dead bushes, jungle temple loot, or fishing.

**Common crafting recipes using sticks:**
- 2 sticks + 3 planks → wooden_sword, wooden_pickaxe, etc. (tool handles)
- 1 stick + 1 coal → 4 torches
- 4 planks + 2 sticks → 3 fences
- 2 planks + 4 sticks → 1 fence_gate
- 7 sticks → 3 ladders
- 3 sticks + 3 string → 1 fishing_rod
- 2 sticks + 3 string → 1 bow
- 1 stick + 1 flint + 1 feather → 4 arrows

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Torch crafting (every blueprint uses torches)
- Fence and gate crafting (farm blueprint perimeter)
- Ladder crafting (castle towers, wizard's tower)
- Tool handle for pickaxes, axes, shovels, hoes

**Related items:** `torch`, `oak_fence`, `oak_fence_gate`, `ladder`, `bow`,
`fishing_rod`, all tool entries
