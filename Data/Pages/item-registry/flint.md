## Notes

Flint is obtained by breaking gravel blocks. Each gravel block has ~10% chance
to drop flint instead of gravel. Fortune enchantment increases this chance
(up to ~100% at Fortune III).

**Gather strategy:** Dig gravel with a shovel. For reliable flint, use a
Fortune-enchanted shovel. Without Fortune, dig ~10 gravel per flint.

**Crafting uses:**
- 1 flint + 1 iron_ingot → 1 flint_and_steel
- 1 flint + 1 stick + 1 feather → 4 arrows
- Flint block is not craftable (flint is item-only, not a block)

**Related items:** `gravel` (source block), `flint_and_steel`, `arrow`
