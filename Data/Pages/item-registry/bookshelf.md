## Notes

A bookshelf is crafted from 6 oak planks + 3 books (2×3 grid) → 1 bookshelf.
Each book requires 1 leather + 3 paper. Paper is crafted from 3 sugar cane.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Mine wood logs for planks (6 planks per bookshelf)
2. Collect sugar cane (grows near water) — craft 3 → 3 paper
3. Hunt cows/rabbits for leather (or craft from rabbit hide × 4)
4. Craft 1 leather + 3 paper → 1 book
5. Craft 6 planks + 3 books → 1 bookshelf

**Phase 4b limitation:** Multiple crafting steps are manual. Bookshelves are
complex to craft from raw materials. The agent may need to source books from
village libraries as an alternative.

**Placement note:** Bookshelves placed adjacent to an enchanting table (1 block gap)
increase enchantment levels. 15 bookshelves max out a level-30 enchantment.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Enchanting room decoration (wizard's tower: 15 bookshelves around enchanting table)
- Library aesthetic
- Note: Bookshelves drop nothing when broken without Silk Touch — use Silk Touch
  or craft replacements
