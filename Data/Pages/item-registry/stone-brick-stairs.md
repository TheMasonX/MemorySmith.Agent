## Notes

Stone brick stairs are crafted from 6 stone bricks (L-shape arrangement) → 4 stairs.
They provide a solid, blast-resistant stair option for medieval builds.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Mine cobblestone → smelt into stone → craft stone bricks
2. Craft 6 stone_bricks → 4 stone_brick_stairs

**Phase 4b limitation:** Smelting and crafting are manual.
Requires FurnaceTool (Phase 4b) and CraftItemTool (Phase 5).

**Placement note:** Stairs can face four directions. For castle battlements and
wall access, stairs should face inward toward the structure center.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Castle wall access and battlements
- Tower interior steps
- Decorative wall tops and crenellations
