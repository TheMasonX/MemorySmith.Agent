## Notes

A book is crafted from 3 paper + 1 leather. Books are the intermediate
component for bookshelves and enchanting tables.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Harvest sugar cane → craft 3 sugar cane → 3 paper
2. Hunt cows/rabbits for leather (or craft rabbit_hide × 4 → leather)
3. Craft 3 paper + 1 leather → 1 book

**Crafting uses:**
- 3 books + 6 planks → 1 bookshelf
- 1 book + 4 obsidian + 2 diamonds → enchanting_table
- 1 book + 1 anvil → enchanted_book (at anvil)

**Phase 4b limitation:** Full supply chain requires sugar cane farming,
animal hunting, and multi-step crafting — all manual.

**Related items:** `paper` (crafted from sugar cane), `leather` (from animals),
`bookshelf`, `enchanting_table`, `book_and_quill`
