## Notes

A diamond pickaxe is crafted from 3 diamonds + 2 sticks. It is required
to mine obsidian (min_harvest_level: 4) and ancient debris.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Mine diamond_ore with an iron pickaxe → diamond
2. Craft 3 diamonds + 2 sticks → 1 diamond_pickaxe

**Stats:**
- Mining level: 4 (can mine obsidian, ancient_debris)
- Durability: 1561 uses
- Mining speed: 8

**Tier progression:** Wood → Stone → Iron → Diamond → Netherite.
Diamond is the standard for end-game mining operations.

**Related items:** `diamond`, `stick`, `iron_pickaxe`, `obsidian`,
`netherite_pickaxe`
