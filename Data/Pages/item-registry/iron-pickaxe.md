## Notes

An iron pickaxe is crafted from 3 iron_ingots + 2 sticks. It is the third
tier of pickaxe, required to mine diamond_ore, gold_ore, redstone_ore,
and emerald_ore.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Mine iron_ore with stone pickaxe → raw_iron → smelt to iron_ingot
2. Craft 3 iron_ingots + 2 sticks → 1 iron_pickaxe

**Stats:**
- Mining level: 3 (can mine diamond, gold, redstone, emerald)
- Durability: 250 uses
- Mining speed: 6 (fastest pre-diamond)

**Tier progression:** Wood → Stone → Iron → Diamond → Netherite.
Iron is the key pickaxe for accessing mid-game resources.

**Related items:** `iron_ingot`, `stick`, `stone_pickaxe`, `diamond_pickaxe`
