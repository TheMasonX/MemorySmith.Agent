## Notes

Redstone dust is obtained by mining redstone ore with an iron pickaxe or better
(min_harvest_level: 3). Each ore block drops 4–5 redstone (more with Fortune).

**Gather strategy:** Mine `redstone_ore` with an iron pickaxe or better.
Found at Y levels below 16, most commonly near bedrock level.

**Uses:**
- Redstone wiring for mechanisms (pistons, doors, traps)
- Crafting: redstone_torch, redstone_repeater, redstone_comparator, clock, compass
- Brewing: awkward potion base (requires blaze_powder)

**Related items:** `redstone_ore` (source block), `redstone_torch`,
`redstone_block`, `redstone_repeater`, `redstone_comparator`
