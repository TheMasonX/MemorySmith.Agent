## Notes

Spruce logs are obtained by chopping spruce trees, found in taiga, old-growth taiga,
and snowy taiga biomes. They function identically to oak logs for most recipes.

**Gather strategy:** Punch or axe any spruce tree trunk.
Spruce trees are tall (often 2×2 in old-growth taiga) and yield many logs.

**Crafting:** 1 spruce_log → 4 spruce_planks.
All logs are interchangeable for plank crafting.

**Biome note:** Spruce logs give the build a darker, medieval look preferred for
wizard's towers and rustic builds.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Dark-accent corner posts (wizard's tower, castle interiors)
- Spruce planks for flooring and bookshelves
- Crafting spruce stairs, slabs, fences, doors
