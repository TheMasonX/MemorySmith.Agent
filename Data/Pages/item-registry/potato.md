## Notes

Potatoes are a crop/food item found in village farms or dropped by zombies
(rare drop). Plant potatoes on farmland to grow more. Each mature plant drops
1–4 potatoes. Baked potatoes (smelted) are more nutritious than raw.

**Gather strategy:**
- Loot village farms (most reliable source)
- Kill zombies (rare ~2.5% drop)
- Plant and grow on farmland for renewable supply

**Nutrition (raw):** 1 hunger (0.5 shanks)
**Nutrition (baked):** 5 hunger (2.5 shanks) — smelt in furnace

**Uses:**
- Food source (better baked)
- Pig breeding with carrot
- Poisonous potato byproduct (~2% chance when harvesting)

**Related items:** `carrot`, `beetroot`, `wheat`, `baked_potato`
