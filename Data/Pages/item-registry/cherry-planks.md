## Notes

Cherry planks are crafted from cherry logs: 1 cherry_log → 4 cherry_planks.
They have a warm brown colour with a slight pinkish tint, introduced in 1.20.

**Gather strategy:** Chop cherry logs from cherry grove trees → craft into planks.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Decorative pink-toned building material
- Romantic or magical garden builds
- Crafting cherry stairs, slabs, doors, fences, trapdoors

**Related items:** `cherry_log`, `oak_planks`, `spruce_planks`
