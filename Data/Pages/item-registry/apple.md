## Notes

Apples are obtained by breaking oak or dark_oak leaves (~0.5% chance each).
They can also be found in village chests and dungeon chests. A golden apple
is crafted from 8 gold_ingots + 1 apple.

**Gather strategy:**
- Chop oak/dark_oak tree leaves → ~0.5% chance per leaf block
- Loot village chests and dungeon chests
- A large oak tree farm provides a steady apple supply

**Nutrition:** 4 hunger (2 shanks).

**Uses:**
- Eating (basic food)
- Golden apple crafting (high-tier combat item)
- Enchanted golden apple is not craftable (loot only)

**Related items:** `golden_apple`, `oak_log`
