## Notes

A fishing rod is crafted from 3 sticks + 2 string. It is used for fishing
in water, which yields fish, treasure, and junk items.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Craft sticks from planks
2. Obtain string from spiders/cobwebs
3. Craft 3 sticks + 2 string → 1 fishing_rod

**Fishing loot (approximate):**
- 85% fish (raw cod, raw salmon, pufferfish, tropical fish)
- 10% junk (sticks, string, bones, etc.)
- 5% treasure (enchanted books, bows, fishing rods, etc.)

**Uses:**
- Food gathering (renewable fish)
- Treasure hunting (enchanted books, name tags, saddles)
- Food source for taming cats and ocelots

**Durability:** 64 casts before breaking.
Can be enchanted with Luck of the Sea, Lure, and Unbreaking.

**Related items:** `stick`, `string`, `raw_cod`, `raw_salmon`
