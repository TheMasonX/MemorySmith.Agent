## Notes

Sand is found in deserts, beaches, rivers, and warm ocean floors. It obeys
gravity — it falls when the block below is removed. Smelting sand in a furnace
produces glass.

**Gather strategy:** Dig with any shovel. Sand drops itself without Silk Touch.
Red sand is functionally identical but cannot be crafted into sandstone variants.

**Smelting:** 1 sand → 1 glass (requires FurnaceTool, Phase 4b).
Then 6 glass → 16 glass_pane at a crafting table.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Glass production (farm blueprint windows, wizard's tower observatory)
- Sandstone building material
- TNT crafting (rare — agent use not expected)
- Concrete powder crafting (with dye)

**Related items:** `glass` (smelted product), `glass_pane` (crafted from glass),
`sandstone`, `red_sand`
