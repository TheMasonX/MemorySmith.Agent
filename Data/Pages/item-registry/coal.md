## Notes

Coal is obtained by mining coal_ore with any pickaxe. Each ore block drops
1 coal (more with Fortune). Coal is primarily used as furnace fuel and for
torch crafting.

**Gather strategy:** Mine `coal_ore` (found at any Y level, most common above
Y=64) with any pickaxe. Each piece smelts 8 items in a furnace.

**Crafting:** 1 coal + 1 stick → 4 torches.

**Alternative:** Charcoal can be used in place of coal for all recipes.
Charcoal is obtained by smelting any log in a furnace.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Torch production (farm, castle, wizard's tower — all use torches)
- Furnace fuel for smelting cobblestone → stone, sand → glass
- Campfire crafting

**Related items:** `charcoal` (renewable alternative), `coal_block` (compact
storage, smelts 80 items), `torch` (crafted from coal + stick)
