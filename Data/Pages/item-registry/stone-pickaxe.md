## Notes

A stone pickaxe is crafted from 3 cobblestone + 2 sticks. It is the second
tier of pickaxe (after wood) and is required to mine iron_ore and lapis_ore.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Mine stone → cobblestone with a wooden pickaxe
2. Craft sticks from planks
3. Craft 3 cobblestone + 2 sticks → 1 stone_pickaxe

**Stats:**
- Mining level: 2 (can mine iron_ore, lapis_ore)
- Durability: 131 uses
- Mining speed: 4 (vs 2 for wood)

**Tier progression:** Wood → Stone → Iron → Diamond → Netherite.
Stone is the minimum viable pickaxe for most mining expeditions.

**Related items:** `cobblestone`, `stick`, `iron_pickaxe`, `wooden_pickaxe`
