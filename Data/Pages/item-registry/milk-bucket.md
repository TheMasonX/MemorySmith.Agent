## Notes

A milk bucket is obtained by using a bucket on a cow, mooshroom, or goat.
Drinking milk clears all status effects (positive and negative). It is also
used for cake crafting.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Craft a bucket from 3 iron_ingots
2. Find a cow, mooshroom, or goat
3. Right-click with empty bucket → milk_bucket

**Uses:**
- Drinking removes all potion effects
- Cake crafting: 3 wheat + 1 sugar + 1 egg + 2 milk_bucket + 1 cocoa_beans
- Cannot be placed as a block (unlike water_bucket)

**Related items:** `bucket`, `water_bucket`, `cake`, `cow`
