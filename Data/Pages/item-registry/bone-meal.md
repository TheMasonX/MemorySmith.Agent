## Notes

Bone meal is crafted from bones: 1 bone → 3 bone_meal. It is primarily used
as a fertiliser to instantly grow crops, trees, flowers, and grass. It also
functions as a white dye.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Kill skeletons for bones
2. Craft 1 bone → 3 bone_meal
3. Alternatively, place and mine a bone_block → 9 bone_meal

**Farming uses:**
- Right-click on crops (wheat, carrots, potatoes) → instantly advances growth
- Right-click on saplings → instantly grows tree
- Right-click on grass block → generates tall grass and flowers
- Right-click on sugar cane → does NOT work (sugar cane ignores bone meal)

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Accelerating wheat growth in farm blueprint
- Tree farming for rapid wood production
- Decorative plant growth around builds

**Related items:** `bone` (raw material), `wheat_seeds`, `wheat`
