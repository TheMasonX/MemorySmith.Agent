## Notes

Melons grow from melon seeds planted on farmland. They spawn on adjacent
dirt/grass blocks when the stem matures. Breaking a melon drops 3–7 melon
slices (9 melon slices → 1 melon block).

**Gather strategy:**
1. Find melon seeds in chests (mineshafts, dungeons) or trade with villagers
2. Plant on farmland, wait for stem growth and melon generation
3. Mine the melon block (drops slices)

**Crafting uses:**
- 9 melon_slices → 1 melon (storage)
- 1 melon_slice → 1 melon_seed
- Melon blocks are decorative and provide food

**Related items:** `melon_slice`, `melon_seeds`, `pumpkin`, `sugar`
