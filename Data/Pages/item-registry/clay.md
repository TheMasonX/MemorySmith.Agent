## Notes

Clay blocks are found underwater in rivers, swamps, and shallow ocean floors.
Breaking a clay block drops 4 clay balls, which can be smelted into bricks
or crafted back into clay blocks.

**Gather strategy:** Dig clay blocks underwater with any shovel.
Each block drops 4 clay balls (not a clay block itself).

**Smelting:** 1 clay ball → 1 brick (requires FurnaceTool, Phase 4b).
4 bricks craft into 1 brick_block.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Brick block production for decorative builds
- Flower pot crafting (3 bricks)
- Not typically used in normal survival builds; requires smelting

**Related items:** `brick` (smelted product), `brick_block`, `flower_pot`
