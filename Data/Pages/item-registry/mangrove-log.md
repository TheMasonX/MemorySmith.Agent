## Notes

Mangrove logs are obtained by chopping mangrove trees (Java 1.19+), found in
mangrove swamp biomes. Mangrove roots grow in water and propagules hang from
the tree canopy.

**Gather strategy:** Chop mangrove tree trunks in mangrove swamps.
Mangrove trees have distinctive roots (mangrove_roots) and propagate via
drooping propagules.

**Crafting:** 1 mangrove_log → 4 mangrove_planks.
All logs are interchangeable for plank crafting.

**Related items:** `mangrove_planks`, `mangrove_roots`, `propagule`
