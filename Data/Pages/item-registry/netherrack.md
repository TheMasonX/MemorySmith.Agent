## Notes

Netherrack is a brittle stone found throughout the Nether. It is easily mined
with any pickaxe and breaks very quickly. Netherrack fires burn indefinitely
when ignited.

**Gather strategy:** Mine `netherrack` in any Nether biome with any pickaxe.
It is the most abundant block in the Nether dimension.

**Uses:**
- Building material for nether-themed builds
- Fuel for eternal fire features (fire on netherrack never goes out)
- Smelting: 2 netherrack + 1 fuel → 1 nether_brick (smelting)
- Gold ore generates embedded in netherrack (nether_gold_ore)

**Related items:** `nether_brick`, `glowstone`, `soul_sand`
