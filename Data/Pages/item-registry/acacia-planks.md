## Notes

Acacia planks are crafted from acacia logs: 1 acacia_log → 4 acacia_planks.
They have a distinctive orange-red colour, unique among wood types.

**Gather strategy:** Chop acacia logs from savanna trees → craft into planks.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Warm orange-red accents
- Savanna-style building aesthetic
- Crafting acacia stairs, slabs, doors, fences, trapdoors

**Related items:** `acacia_log`, `oak_planks`, `spruce_planks`
