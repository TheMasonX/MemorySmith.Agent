## Notes

A bow is crafted from 3 sticks + 3 string. It is a ranged weapon that
fires arrows. Can be enchanted with Power, Flame, Infinity, Punch.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Craft sticks from planks
2. Obtain string from spiders or cobwebs
3. Craft 3 sticks + 3 string → 1 bow

**Uses:**
- Ranged combat (skeletons, creepers, etc.)
- Hunting animals for food and leather
- Activating targets and tripwire circuits

**Durability:** 384 shots before breaking.

**Related items:** `arrow`, `string`, `stick`, `crossbow`
