## Notes

Birch planks are crafted from birch logs: 1 birch_log → 4 birch_planks.
They have a pale yellow-white colour, ideal for bright modern builds.

**Gather strategy:** Chop birch logs from birch trees → craft into planks.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Bright flooring and wall panelling
- Modern and minimalist build aesthetic
- Crafting birch stairs, slabs, doors, fences, trapdoors

**Related items:** `birch_log`, `oak_planks`, `spruce_planks`
