## Notes

Sugar is obtained by smelting sugar cane in a furnace. It is used as a
crafting ingredient for cakes, pumpkin pies, and fermented spider eyes.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Harvest sugar cane (grows near water)
2. Smelt 1 sugar_cane → 1 sugar

**Crafting uses:**
- 3 wheat + 1 sugar + 1 egg + 2 milk_bucket + 1 cocoa_beans → 1 cake
- 1 pumpkin + 1 sugar + 1 egg → 1 pumpkin_pie
- 1 brown_mushroom + 1 sugar + 1 spider_eye → 1 fermented_spider_eye

**Related items:** `sugar_cane`, `cake`, `pumpkin_pie`
