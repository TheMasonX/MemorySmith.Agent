## Notes

Dirt is one of the most abundant blocks in Minecraft, found in nearly all
surface biomes. It can be dug with any tool (or bare hands), though a shovel
is fastest. When tilled with a hoe, dirt becomes farmland.

**Gather strategy:** Dig any grass or dirt block with a shovel for maximum speed.
Drops itself without needing Silk Touch.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Tilled farmland base for crop plots (farm blueprint: 121 dirt blocks at Y=0)
- Terraforming and landscaping
- Path creation (can be converted to dirt_path with a shovel)

**Related items:** `farmland` (tilled state), `grass_block` (living variant),
`coarse_dirt`, `rooted_dirt`
