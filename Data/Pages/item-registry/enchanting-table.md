## Notes

An enchanting table is crafted from 4 obsidian + 2 diamonds + 1 book → 1 enchanting table.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Mine diamond_ore with an iron (or better) pickaxe → diamond (min_harvest_level: 2)
2. Mine obsidian with a diamond pickaxe (min_harvest_level: 3) — requires water + lava source
3. Craft book from leather + paper (3 sugar cane → 3 paper)
4. Craft: obsidian (bottom row + corners) + diamonds (middle row sides) + book (center)

**Phase 4b limitation:** Requires diamond-level tools and obsidian mining.
All crafting and smelting steps are manual. This is a mid-to-late-game item.

**Placement note:** The enchanting table occupies 1 block. Bookshelves placed
within a 5×5 ring (1 block gap from the table) boost enchantment power up to
level 30 with 15 bookshelves.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Centerpiece of wizard's tower enchanting room
- End-game enchanting station
