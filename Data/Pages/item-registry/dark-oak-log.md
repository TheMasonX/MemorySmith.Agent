## Notes

Dark oak logs are obtained by chopping dark oak trees, found in dark forest
biomes. Dark oak trees are thick (2×2 trunk) and generate with a dense canopy
that blocks light — hostile mobs spawn under them.

**Gather strategy:** Chop 2×2 dark oak tree trunks. Watch for mobs under
the dark canopy. Dark oak logs have a darker brown bark.

**Crafting:** 1 dark_oak_log → 4 dark_oak_planks.
All logs are interchangeable for plank crafting.

**Related items:** `dark_oak_planks`, `oak_log`, `spruce_log`
