## Notes

Lapis lazuli is obtained by mining lapis_ore with a stone pickaxe or better.
Each ore block drops 4–9 lapis (more with Fortune). It is used primarily for
enchanting (as the fuel for enchanting tables) and as a blue dye.

**Gather strategy:** Mine `lapis_ore` with a stone pickaxe or better.
Found at Y levels below 64, most commonly at Y=0.

**Uses:**
- Enchanting table fuel (1–3 lapis per enchant, depending on level)
- Blue dye (craft with wool for blue wool, or directly as dye)
- Decorative block crafting (9 lapis → 1 lapis_block)

**Related items:** `enchanting_table`, `lapis_block`, `blue_dye`
