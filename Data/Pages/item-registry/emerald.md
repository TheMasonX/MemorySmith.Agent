## Notes

Emeralds are obtained by mining emerald_ore with an iron pickaxe or better.
They are the primary currency for villager trading.

**Gather strategy:** Mine `emerald_ore` with an iron pickaxe or better.
Emeralds generate only in mountain biomes at Y levels above 0, most
commonly at Y=236.

**Uses:**
- Villager trading currency (buy/sell items with villagers)
- Beacon base material (9 emeralds → 1 emerald_block)
- Fletching table crafting (4 planks + 2 flint)

**Related items:** `emerald_block`, `villager` (trading partner)
