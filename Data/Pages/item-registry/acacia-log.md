## Notes

Acacia logs are obtained by chopping acacia trees, found in savanna biomes.
Acacia trees have a distinctive flat-topped canopy and orange-grey bark.

**Gather strategy:** Chop acacia tree trunks in savanna biomes. Acacia trees
are short to medium height with unique flat canopy shapes.

**Crafting:** 1 acacia_log → 4 acacia_planks.
All logs are interchangeable for plank crafting.

**Related items:** `acacia_planks`, `oak_log`, `spruce_log`
