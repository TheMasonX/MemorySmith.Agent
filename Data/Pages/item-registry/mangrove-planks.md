## Notes

Mangrove planks are crafted from mangrove logs: 1 mangrove_log → 4 mangrove_planks.
They have a rich teal-brown colour introduced in 1.19.

**Gather strategy:** Chop mangrove logs from mangrove swamp trees → craft into planks.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Swamp-themed builds
- Unique teal-toned accents
- Crafting mangrove stairs, slabs, doors, fences, trapdoors

**Related items:** `mangrove_log`, `oak_planks`, `spruce_planks`
