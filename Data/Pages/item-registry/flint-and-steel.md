# flint-and-steel

item_id: flint_and_steel
display_name: Flint and Steel
source_blocks: iron_ore, deepslate_iron_ore, gravel
requires_smelting: true
min_harvest_level: 2

## Notes

Flint and steel is crafted from 1 iron_ingot + 1 flint. It is used to
ignite fires, activate nether portals, and ignite creepers/TNT.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Smelt iron_ore → iron_ingot; dig gravel → flint
2. Craft 1 iron_ingot + 1 flint → 1 flint_and_steel

**Uses:**
- Right-click on nether portal frame → activates portal
- Right-click on solid block → creates fire block
- Right-click on TNT → ignites explosion
- Right-click on campfire → lights it

**Durability:** 64 uses before breaking. Cannot be enchanted.

**Related items:** `iron_ingot`, `flint`, `fire_charge` (alternative)
