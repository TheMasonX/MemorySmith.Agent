## Notes

Coal ore is the most common ore in Minecraft, found at all Y levels below
Y=256. It generates in large veins and is the primary source of coal.

**Gather strategy:** Mine with any pickaxe. Wooden pickaxe is sufficient
(min_harvest_level: 1). Drops 1 coal (more with Fortune enchantment).

**Best mining levels:** Coal generates most commonly above Y=64 (mountain
exposures) and between Y=0 and Y=128. Deepslate variant generates below Y=0.

**Related items:** `coal` (dropped item), `charcoal` (renewable alternative)
