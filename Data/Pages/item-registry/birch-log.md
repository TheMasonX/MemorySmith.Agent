## Notes

Birch logs are obtained by chopping birch trees, found in birch forests, old-growth
birch forests, and mixed forests. Birch has a distinctive white bark.

**Gather strategy:** Punch or axe any birch tree trunk. Birch trees are
medium height with white-grey bark and dark branches.

**Crafting:** 1 birch_log → 4 birch_planks.
All logs are interchangeable for plank crafting.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Light-coloured building accents (contrasts with oak/spruce)
- Birch plank crafting for modern builds
- Decorative stripped birch log for pale structures

**Related items:** `birch_planks`, `oak_log`, `spruce_log`
