## Notes

Feathers are obtained by killing chickens (0–2 feathers per kill). Parrots drop
1–2 feathers. Phantoms drop 0–1 phantom membrane (not feathers). Feathers are
used to craft arrows and books.

**Gather strategy:** Hunt chickens. They are common in all grassy biomes and
easy to kill. A chicken farm provides a renewable source.

**Crafting uses:**
- 1 flint + 1 stick + 1 feather → 4 arrows
- Book and quill (1 book + 1 feather + 1 ink_sac)

**Related items:** `arrow` (crafted with feather), `chicken`, `flint`, `stick`
