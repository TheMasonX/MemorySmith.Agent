## Notes

Dark oak planks are crafted from dark oak logs: 1 dark_oak_log → 4 dark_oak_planks.
They are the darkest naturally occurring planks, ideal for medieval and Gothic builds.

**Gather strategy:** Chop dark oak logs from dark forest trees → craft into planks.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Dark accent walls and trim (Gothic/magical builds)
- Contrast with lighter planks for detailing
- Crafting dark_oak stairs, slabs, doors, fences, trapdoors

**Related items:** `dark_oak_log`, `spruce_planks`, `oak_planks`
