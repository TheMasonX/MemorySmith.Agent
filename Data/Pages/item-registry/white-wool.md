## Notes

White wool is obtained by shearing sheep (1–3 wool per sheep) or killing them
(1 wool drop). Sheep regrow wool by eating grass. Any colour of wool can be
used for bed crafting — the item name reflects the colour used.

**Gather strategy:**
- Find sheep in grassy biomes
- Use shears on a sheep → 1–3 white_wool (safer, allows regrowth)
- Or kill the sheep for 1 wool
- Dye can be applied to change wool colour

**Crafting uses:**
- 3 wool + 3 planks → 1 bed (any colour wool works)
- Wool blocks for decoration, carpets, and banners
- Note block (8 planks + 1 wool)

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Bed crafting (small-house, farm, wizard's tower all use beds)
- Decorative flooring and roofing

**Related items:** `red_bed` (crafted from planks + wool), `shears`,
`string` (alternative — 4 string → 1 wool, from spiders)
