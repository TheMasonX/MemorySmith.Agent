## Notes

Gravel is found underwater, in rivers, beaches, and underground gravel patches.
It obeys gravity and falls when unsupported. Breaking gravel has a ~10% chance
to drop flint instead of gravel.

**Gather strategy:** Dig with any shovel. Each broken block has a chance to drop
flint. For reliable flint, use a Fortune-enchanted shovel.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Path and road surfacing
- Concrete powder crafting (with sand and dye)
- Flint source for flint_and_steel and arrow crafting

**Related items:** `flint` (byproduct), `sand`, `concrete_powder`
