## Notes

A block of iron is crafted from 9 iron_ingots (3×3 arrangement). It is a
decorative block, beacon base material, and compact iron storage. Iron blocks
can be uncrafted back into 9 iron_ingots.

**Gather strategy:** Craft 9 iron_ingots → 1 iron_block. Iron blocks are
also found in some generated structures (woodland mansions, buried treasure).

**Uses:**
- Beacon pyramid base
- Compact ingot storage
- Decorative industrial/military builds
- Anvil base ingredient (3 iron_blocks + 4 iron_ingots → 1 anvil)

**Related items:** `iron_ingot`, `gold_block`, `diamond_block`, `beacon`, `anvil`
