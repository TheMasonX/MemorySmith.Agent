## Notes

Jungle planks are crafted from jungle logs: 1 jungle_log → 4 jungle_planks.
They have a warm brown colour with a slightly reddish tint.

**Gather strategy:** Chop jungle logs from jungle trees → craft into planks.

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Warm-toned building material
- Exotic decorative accents
- Crafting jungle stairs, slabs, doors, fences, trapdoors

**Related items:** `jungle_log`, `oak_planks`, `spruce_planks`
