## Notes

Sugar cane is a plant that grows up to 4 blocks tall next to water. It is
found in nearly all biomes along riverbanks and shorelines. Break the top
blocks to harvest while leaving the bottom for regrowth.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Find sugar cane growing naturally near water
2. Break blocks above the bottom block → sugar_cane drops
3. The bottom block continues growing, producing more cane over time

**Crafting:**
- 3 sugar cane → 3 paper (paper.md)
- 1 sugar cane → 1 sugar (in furnace, for cake/pumpkin pie)

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Paper production for books (wizard's tower: ~66 books = 198 paper = 198 sugar cane)
- Book crafting requires extensive sugar cane farming
- Decorative element in builds

**Related items:** `paper` (crafted product), `book` (paper + leather),
`bookshelf` (books + planks), `sugar` (smelted)
