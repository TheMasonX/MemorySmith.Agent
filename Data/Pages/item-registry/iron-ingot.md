## Notes

Iron ingots are obtained by smelting raw iron (or iron ore in older versions)
in a furnace. They are the most versatile metal in the early-to-mid game,
used for tools, armour, buckets, shears, and iron blocks.

**Gather strategy:**
1. Mine `iron_ore` → `raw_iron` (stone pickaxe or better)
2. Smelt 1 raw_iron → 1 iron_ingot (requires FurnaceTool, Phase 4b)

**Crafting uses:**
- 3 iron_ingots → 1 bucket (bucket.md)
- 3 iron_ingots → 1 shears (shears.md)
- 2 iron_ingots → 1 sword, 3 → pickaxe, etc. (tool entries)
- 9 iron_ingots → 1 iron_block (compact storage)
- 6 iron_ingots → 16 iron_bars (iron-bars.md)

**Common uses in blueprints:**
- Bucket creation for farm irrigation (farm blueprint)
- Iron bar crafting for castle window grates
- Tool crafting for mining and combat

**Related items:** `raw_iron` (raw material), `iron_ore` (source block),
`iron_block`, `iron_bars`, `bucket`, `shears`
